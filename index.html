ConSoul

Access to affordable therapy can be nearly impossible. ConSoul gives people in need the opportunity to recieve the care they deserve. Mental health shouldn't come at a price. Through ConSoul you are connected with pre-licensed therapists who need to meet their hourly quota, by giving voluntary (free) therapy. This is a powerful way of teaming up and helping one another accomplish common goals.
