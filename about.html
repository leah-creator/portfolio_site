My Story

Hello. I'm Leah, I am a UX designer with a specialization in Frontend Development. My interest in UX began 2 years ago when I was home alone, isolated during the pandemic. I live in Los Angeles and was surrounded by people struggling with depression, anxiety and self harm. I desperately wanted to find a way to help connect people and address mental-health while still taking precautions to stay home. My history in the film industry relied on an expertise in psychology, creativity, storytelling and empathy. This seemed like a perfect fit for a UX Career and so began my journey. My passions for mental health and creativity lead me to design two apps, one regarding mental-health. The app I designed landed me a job as UX intern at a company for 3+ months. I worked along side other UX designers on projects for teenagers partnering with SEL programs. Social, Emotional Learning. Working as a designer deepened my passion and enhanced my skills to work on creating products that provide user-centered solutions. My background as an actress has taught me the value of empathy, story telling, quickly improvising to create solutions, being a team player and working in a fast paced environment.

When I am not designing- I am probably painting, hiking or writing music. Or possibly dog-sitting because I am obsessed with animals.
